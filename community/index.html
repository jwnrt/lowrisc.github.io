Community

Keeping up to date

The best ways of keeping up with lowRISC developments are:

  • Checking the blog, and subscribing to the RSS feed.
  • Signing up to our announcement mailing list.
  • Following @lowRISC on Twitter.

Mailing lists and IRC

Development discussion takes place on the lowrisc-dev mailing list. Archives are here and you can subscribe here. There’s also relevant discussion on the RISC-V mailing lists.

We also have an IRC channel where a number of us as well others interested in lowRISC, RISC-V, and open source hardware hang out. There won’t always be someone active, so if you have a support question you may be better off posting to the mailing list. Join #lowRISC on irc.oftc.net.
